Materials specifically for interview rehearsal and final-stage prep.

## How to use

These files are for the final week before an interview. Don't drill them daily — pull them out for:
- Final rehearsal in the 7 days before
- A weekly mock to practice answering out loud
- After each real interview, capture the questions you got and add them here

The STAR stories and troubleshooting drills should be **memorized to the TL;DR level** so you can answer cleanly under pressure.
